Hi! I'm Jordan 👋

I am a molecular biologist turned bioinformatician and AI engineer based in Cambridge, UK. My work spans fungal biotechnology, functional genomics, computational biology, and applied AI. I build bioinformatics pipelines, genome analysis workflows, and AI-assisted research tools that help turn complex biological data and literature into useful scientific insight.

What I work on:
- Functional and comparative genomics
- Fungal biotechnology and microbial genomics
- Genome assembly, annotation, and analysis
- Reproducible bioinformatics pipelines
- AI-assisted research tools and natural-language interfaces to data
